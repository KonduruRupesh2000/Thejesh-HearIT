About Us

As a group of four, we created this website with the goal of understanding a person's feelings through their speech. Artificial intelligence (AI) systems are predicted to rely on the accurate identification of human emotions from data sources such as conversation content, voice, and facial expressions in a growing variety of applications. Audio player and emotion detection are key components of our project, which will present users with personalised music suggestions based on their speech analysis-detected emotions.

  • A key goal of our study is to detect a person's emotional state through their speech and then use music to lift their Emotions.
  • Hear It uses music's innate mood-lifting benefits to assist individuals enhance their mental health and overall well-being.
  • Personalized care systems can urge people to rest when they are fatigued, while self-driving cars can slow down when they sense scared feelings.
